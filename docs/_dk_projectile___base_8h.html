strong
Enumerator
OnHit 

Damage is applied once upon hitting a target.

Projectiles with this policy will be destroyed upon hitting a target, and can be blocked.

OnBeginOverlap 

Damage is applied multiple times upon beginning overlap with multiple targets.

Projectiles with this policy will not be destroyed upon hitting a target, and cannot be blocked.
