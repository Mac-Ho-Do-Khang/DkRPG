Detailed Description

Implements calculation logic related to damage taken by both the player and enemy characters.

Four calculations are implemented within this class:

  • CalculateDamage() How much damage to deal, based on attacker's base damage, attack power, target's defense power and combo multipliers
  • CalculateStaminaDeduction() How much stamina to deduct when blocking an attack, based on the amount of damage blocked
  • CalculateHealthWithered() How much wither health to apply when blocking an attack, based on the amount of damage blocked
  • CalculateHealthRecovered() How much wither health to recover when dealing damage, based on the amount of damage dealt
Note
When applying damage to Player Character, this class is executed twice:
  • When the damage GE is applied to the player character, this class applies health reduction if the attack is not being blocked or if the player doesn't have enough stamina to block the attack
  • When the player character's blocking GA receives the event that the attack is successfully blocked, this class applies stamina reduction & health withering if it is not a perfect block (i.e. a parry).
When applying damage to Enemy Characters, this class is executed twice:
  • When the damage GE is applied to an enemy character, this class applies health reduction
  • Then, apply on the player character to recover wither health based on the damage dealt
