Detailed Description

Implements calculation logic related to wither health recovery. Applied to player character only.

When the player character succsessfully blocks an enemy attack, their stamina is deducted and a portion of health is withered. This withered health can be recover by dealing damage to enemies, and will be lost upon taking damage.

One calculation is implemented within this class:

  • How much wither health to recover, based on the amount of damage dealt.
